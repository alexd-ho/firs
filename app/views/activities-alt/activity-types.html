About the activities

Which types of political influence activity must be registered as part of this arrangement?

We will ask you about each of these three types of activity, in turn. For each type of activity, you can add multiple instances of that activities.

Private communication

For example, lobbying, sending an email or letter, or having a private conversation.

Public communication

For example, social media content, poster, leaflet, or published article.

Distribution of money, goods or services

For example, the offer of money, goods or services to an individual or entity in the UK.
