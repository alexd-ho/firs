Registering an arrangement for a registered company

To register this arrangment you'll need:

  • the company name, any trading names, registered address, company number and date and country of incorporation
  • your name and contact details
  • the name and email address of a senior responsible officer in the company

Proof of identity

As part of this registration, we'll ask you for the name and email address of a senior officer in the company. This is so they can prove their identity for security reasons.

A senior officer is someone who has authority to make decisions on behalf of the company, such as a Chief Executive Officer (CEO), or managing director.

We'll send them details explaining how to prove their identity using GOV.UK One Login. You won't need to do anything else.

We'll let you know once they have proven their identity and your registration has been processed.
