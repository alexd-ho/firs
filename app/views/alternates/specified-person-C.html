Information we will show on the public register

The information you provide in the next section will be shown on the public register unless it:

  • puts you or someone else's safety at risk
  • puts UK national security at risk
  • contains details that could harm a business or industry

The public register helps to inform the public about foreign influence in the UK.

After each question in this section we will ask if you have any concerns about the information you have provided.

If you do have concerns we will ask you to give details and provide supporting evidence.

We will contact you about your concerns once you submit your registration.

We will not show any information on the public register before we have contacted you about your concerns.
