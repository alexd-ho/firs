How to register

Check if you need to register before you start.

You must register a new arrangement within 10 days, starting from the day the arrangement is made and before any activities are carried out.

For example, if an arrangement is made on 1 January, you must register by 10 January. If activities are to take place between 1 and 10 January, you must register the arrangement prior to any activities taking place.

You can register online or using a paper form. It's quicker to register online.

Registration is free.

You'll need to register each arrangement you have with a foreign government, a sovereign, or head of state separately. You not need to register each individual activity carried out as part of an arrangement.

Register online

You'll need:

  • the name of the foreign government, sovereign, or head of state that instructed you to carry out an activity
  • to describe the activities you have agreed to carry out in detail
  • the start and end dates of the activities
  • details of the individuals who will carry out the activities
  • the address and contact details of the person if you are registering on behalf of someone else

Start now

Other ways to register

If you cannot register online you can:

  • download and print a paper form
  • phone the FIRS Case Management Team and ask for a form to be posted to you - allow 7 days for the form to arrive

Return your completed form by post.
