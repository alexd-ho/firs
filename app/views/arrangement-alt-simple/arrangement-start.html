How to register

Check if you need to register before you start.

You can register online or using a paper form. It's quicker to register online.

Registration is free.

You'll need to register each arrangement separately. You'll be able to update the details of any activities after you have registered.

Register online

You'll need:

  • the name of the foreign government or local authority, Sovereign, or Head of State that directed you to carry out an activity
  • details of the arrangement
  • details of the activities that will be carried out as part of the arrangement
  • details of the individuals who will carry out the activities
  • the start and end dates of the activities
  • your address and contact details (if you are registering on behalf of someone else you'll need their details)

Start now

Other ways to register

If you cannot register online you can:

  • download and print a paper form
  • phone the FIRS Case Management Team and ask for a form to be posted to you - allow 7 days for the form to arrive

Return your completed form by post.
