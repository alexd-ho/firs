Research round 1

Research w/c 22 January 2024

We are testing the eligibility journey for public registrants.

More about this research
  • Research plan
  • Discussion guide
  • Research analysis

Research aims

  1. What are users' needs, frustrations and concerns - do they align with our discovery personas?
  2. How will users become aware of FIRS without an information notice or personal search?
  3. Are users confident that the service is for them or not and how easy was it for them to know?
  4. Does the eligibility form give a clear answer on next steps and is the length of time it takes to complete acceptable?
