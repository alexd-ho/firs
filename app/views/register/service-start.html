Use this service to register an arrangement on the Foreign Influence Registration Scheme:

  • for yourself
  • for someone else, such as someone that works for your organistion

Registering takes around 30 minutes. You can save your progress as you go.

Before you start

Prove your identity

As part of your application, you can choose to prove your identity.

This may help us process your registration quicker and help us with any future enquiries.

What you'll need to prove your identity

You'll need one of these documents:

  • Passport
  • National identity card
  • Biometric residence card
  • Biometric residence permit

If you are not in the UK, your passport or national identity card must have a biometric chip.
