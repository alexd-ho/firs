How to prove your identity

To help us confirm your identity, we need to check your identity document.

You can:

  • use the 'GOV.UK ID Check' iPhone app or Android app on your phone or someone else's
  • enter your document details online and send your identity document by post

If you send your identity document by post, we will return it by Royal Mail Signed For 2nd Class delivery.

After your identity document has been checked

Using details from your identity document, we will create your Foreign Influence Registration Scheme (FIRS) account and send you a code to access it.

You will need to sign in to complete your registration.

You can complete your registration on a computer or any device.
