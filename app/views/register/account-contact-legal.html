Terms of registering an arrangement for someone else

By registering an arrangment on behalf of someone else you'll need to:

  • agree for us to contact you about the registration
  • confirm the information you give is correct
  • give any additional information related to a registration if we need it
  • give your contact details so that we can contact you about a registration should we need to
  • read the terms and conditions
