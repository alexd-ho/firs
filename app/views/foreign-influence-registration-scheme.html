Overview

If you arrange to carry out certain activities on behalf of a foreign government in the UK you may have to register on the Foreign Influence Registration Scheme (FIRS).

FIRS keeps a Public Register of all activities that are directed by foreign countries and intended to influence UK politics.

You must register a new arrangement within 10 days, starting from the day the arrangement is made and before any activities are carried out.

For example, if an arrangement is made on 1 January, you must register by 10 January. If activities are to take place between 1 and 10 January, you must register the arrangement prior to any activities taking place.

You may not need to register if you are carrying out certain legal, diplomatic, or journalism activities.

This guide is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
