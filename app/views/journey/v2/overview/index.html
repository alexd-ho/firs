Overview

If you arrange or carry out certain activities on behalf of a foreign power in the UK you will need to register on the Foreign Influence Registration Scheme (FIRS).

FIRS keeps a Public Register of activities that are arranged and directed by a foreign power and intended to influence UK politics.

You must register an arrangement within a certain timeframe, depending on the types of activities involved.

You may not need to register if you are carrying out certain legal, diplomatic, or journalism activities.
