How to register

Check if you need to register before you start.

You can register online or using a paper form. It's quicker to register online.

Registration is free.

You must register each agreement, and its associated activities, separately. You can update the details of any activities after you have registered them.

{% from "govuk/components/details/macro.njk" import govukDetails %} {{ govukDetails({ summaryText: "What is an agreement?", text: "An agreement can be anything from a legally enforceable contract to a written or verbal understanding between two or more parties." }) }} {{ govukDetails({ summaryText: "What is an activity?", text: "An activity is any action taken that relates to an agreement. This includes public communications, private communications, and the disbursement of goods and services." }) }}

Register online

You will need:

  • details of the agreement
  • details of the activities that will be carried out as part of the agreement
  • the name of the foreign government or local authority, Sovereign, or Head of State that directed you to carry out the activities
  • details of the individuals who will carry out the activities
  • the start and end dates of the activities
  • your address and contact details (if you are registering on behalf of someone else, you will need their details)
