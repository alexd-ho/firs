Conditions for regsitering and exemptions

When you may not need to register

You may not need to register if:

  • the UK Government is a party to your arrangement
  • you are a foreign government or local authority, Sovereign, or Head of State
  • you are a diplomat or a spouse, partner or family member of a diplomat
  • you are carrying out activities that are reasonably necessary for the functioning of a diplomatic mission
  • you are a recognised news publisher
  • you are a lawyer or solicitor carrying out legal activity
