Add your contact details

You must provide your contact information to use the Foreign Influence Registration Scheme (FIRS).

We need your:

  • name
  • email address
  • telephone number

These should be your contact details, not those of an entity or anyone else.

How we will use your information

We will only use this information to contact you.

We will usually contact you by email. We will only use your telephone number if we cannot contact you by email.

Your contact information will not be shared outside of this service, and will not be made public.

If you are registering an entity

You still need to provide your contact details.

You will be asked to provide the details of the entity.

You can add other users later.
