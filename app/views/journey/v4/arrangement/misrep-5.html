Tell us about the foreign power you work for

You only need to register if, as part of your work, you are being directed to do, or organise for others to do, a political influence activity. An activity is considered a political influence activity if It meets the criteria set out in section 70 of the National Security Act 2023.

You do not need to register an arrangement with a foreign power-controlled entity that has not been specified by the UK government.
