Add an agreement

What you need to complete this section

In this section, we are asking about an agreement with a foreign power or specified foreign power-controlled organisation. The agreement only needs to be registered if it involves activities you are doing or organising others to do in the UK.

You need to know:

  • Who you have an agreement with
  • How the agreement was made
  • When the agreement was made

Some of the information you provide may appear on the Foreign Influence Registration Scheme public register if you register any activities that are considered political in nature.

You must register agreements one at a time.

You can register or amend an agreement at any time, but you must comply with the timescales set out in the Foreign Influence Registration Scheme legislation.
