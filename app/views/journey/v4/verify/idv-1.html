Assign a Senior Responsible Officer

You must assign a Senior Responsible Officer (SRO) for your entity.

{% from 'govuk/components/details/macro.njk' import govukDetails %} {{ govukDetails({ summaryText: 'What is a Senior Responsible Officer?', text: 'A Senior Responsible Officer (SRO) is a person who is responsible for ensuring compliance with the Foreign Influence Registration Scheme (FIRS) within an entity. The SRO is typically a senior executive within the entity who has the authority to make decisions about FIRS and is the single point of contact for the FIRS team. ' }) }}

You need to either provide your details if your are the SRO, or nominate someone else by entering their email address.

They must use the same email address that received the nominate link.

The SRO will need to:

  • verify their identity (ID) using GOV.UK One Login
  • upload a scan or photo of an ID document for our own records

They also need to provide their:

  • full name and any previous names
  • date of birth
  • nationality
  • address
