Verify your identity

We need to verify your identity (ID) to:

  • prevent fraudulent registrations from being made in your name
  • add other people to manage your registrations

What you need to complete this section

There are two stages to verifying your ID:

  • verify your ID on GOV.UK One Login
  • upload your ID to the Foreign Influence Registration Scheme (FIRS) team

If you've already verified your ID using the GOV.UK One Login verification on this service or another service, you will go straight to stage 2 to upload your ID to the FIRS team.

You’ll need a form of ID.

The GOV.UK One Login service will explain what you need and what you need to do.

When you have finished verifying your ID on GOV.UK One Login, you will be redirected back here to your upload your ID to the FIRS team.

Click ‘Go to the GOV.UK One Login’.

The public register

Your ID documents will never appear on the public register.

Read the:

  • guidance on what information appears on the public register
  • guidance on requesting information to remain private
