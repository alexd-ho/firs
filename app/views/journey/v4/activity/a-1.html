What you need to complete this section

In this section, we are asking for information about a single activity that happens as part of the [insert arrangement name] arrangement.

You need to know:

  • what the activity is intended to influence
  • the actions involved in the activity
  • how often the activity happens
  • what you want the activity to achieve
  • who is involved the activity

The public register

If this activity is considered political in nature, some of the information you provide may appear on the Foreign Influence Registration Scheme public register.

You can request for any information that would appear on the public register to remain private.

Read the:

  • guidance on what information appears on the public register
  • guidance on requesting information to remain private
