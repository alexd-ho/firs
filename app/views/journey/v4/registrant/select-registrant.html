Add a registrant

To start a new registration, you must add a new registrant or select one you have already added or have access to.

Add a new registrant

If you are the registrant, you must:

  • provide your personal details
  • verify your identity using our verification service

You cannot register another individual.

If an entity, such as a company, is the registrant, you must:

  • provide details about the entity
  • nominate a senior responsible officer to verify their identity

You can only register an entity if you are either:

  • a Senior Responsible Officer for the entity
  • someone who is formally part of the entity, such as an employee, member or volunteer, and has been given permission to register it

You can save your progress at any point and return later.
