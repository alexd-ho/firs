Add your entity details

You must provide the details of your entity.

What you need to complete this section

You need to provide:

  • the name of your entity, and any other names used by it
  • where your entity was incorporated or formed
  • the date your entity was incorporated or formed
  • the address of your entity

You will be the primary contact for your entity. You cannot reassign this role until you have submitted your first registration.

After submission, if we are able to verify the identity of your entity, you will be given permission to add other users and reassign roles.

The public register

If you add a political influencing activity, some of the information related to this registration will appear on the Foreign Influence Registration Scheme public register.

Information set to appear on the public register may remain private if:

  • publication could cause serious harm to any individual involved in this activity
  • publication could harm a criminal investigation or proceedings, or prevent the detection of crime
  • publication could harm the safety or interests of the UK
  • publication could harm the commercial interests of any individual or entity in this activity

At the 'Check and submit your registration' stage, you can tell us if you believe any of the registration information creates one or more of these risks and should stay private. If you do, you will have 5 days to present your case.

Read the:

  • guidance on what information appears on the public register
  • guidance on requesting information to remain private
