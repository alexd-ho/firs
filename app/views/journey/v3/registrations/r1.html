  • Who you have an agreement with
  • How the agreement was made
  • When the agreement was made

Some of the information you provide may appear on the Foreign Influence Registration Scheme public register if you register any activities that are considered political in nature.

You must register agreements one at a time.

You can register or amend an agreement at any time, but you must comply with the timescales set out in the Foreign Influence Registration Scheme (FIRS) legislation.
