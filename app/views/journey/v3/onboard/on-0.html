You have now created your Foreign Influence Registration Scheme (FIRS) account.

To begin, we need to determine what type of registrant you are.
There are four types of registrant:

  • Individual who is conducting or organising activities in the UK as part of an agreement with a foreign power or specified organisation
  • Organisation that is conducting or organising activities in the UK as part of an agreement with a foreign power or specified organisation
  • Specified organisation conducting or organising activities in the UK
  • Individual who has a role, position or affiliation with a foreign power, conducting or organising activities in the UK, and who inaccurately reports or 'misrepresents' their relationship with the foreign power or the activities in the UK
